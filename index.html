Our Professional Services

Interior Painting

Transform any room in your home or business with our professional interior painting services. We use premium paints and proven techniques to deliver flawless, long-lasting finishes. Whether it's a single accent wall or your entire property, we handle projects of all sizes with the same attention to detail.

Drywall Services

From new installations to repairs, our drywall experts ensure smooth, seamless walls ready for painting. We handle everything from patching small holes to complete room installations, always focusing on creating the perfect canvas for your paint.

Plastering & Finishing

Our skilled team provides traditional plastering services and modern finishing techniques. We repair cracks, smooth rough surfaces, and prepare walls for the perfect paint application. Every surface is meticulously prepared to ensure professional results.

Specialty Projects

Garage finishing is one of our specialties - transforming cold, unfinished spaces into clean, usable areas. We also handle basement painting, commercial spaces, and custom projects. Whatever your vision, we'll help bring it to life.

About Brush and Beyond Painting

Our Story

Brush and Beyond Painting was founded on a simple principle: deliver exceptional painting services while treating every home as if it were our own. Led by Bailey, our team has built a reputation in Ottawa for quality work, honest communication, and reliable service.

What Sets Us Apart

Speed Without Compromise - We complete projects efficiently to get you back to normal life faster, without sacrificing quality. Our experienced team knows how to work quickly while maintaining the highest standards.

Meticulous Cleanliness - We understand that having work done in your home can be disruptive. That's why we use advanced dust barriers, protect your belongings, and clean up thoroughly each day. Many clients are amazed that their space is cleaner after we leave than before we arrived.

Direct Communication - When you work with us, you're not dealing with a sales team or middleman. Bailey personally oversees every project, ensuring clear communication from quote to completion. Quick responses, honest advice, and regular updates are standard.

Fair, Transparent Pricing - No games, no hidden fees, no surprise charges. Our quotes are detailed and honest. We believe in fair pricing for quality work, and our customers appreciate knowing exactly what they're paying for.

Our Commitment

Every project, whether it's a single room or an entire home, receives our full attention and commitment to excellence. We're not satisfied until you're thrilled with the results. This dedication to customer satisfaction has made us one of Ottawa's most trusted painting services.

Looking Forward

As we grow, we're excited to expand our services to better serve the Ottawa community. But no matter how much we grow, we'll never lose sight of what matters most: delivering beautiful results, treating your home with respect, and earning your trust through every interaction.
